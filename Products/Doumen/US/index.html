Privacy Policy

Doumen is a local-first bead pattern maker and editor. The v0.1 core workflow works without registration, login, or image uploads.

1. Data We Collect

By default, Doumen v0.1 does not collect personally identifiable information, does not create user accounts, does not require login, and does not upload imported images, project files, or exported patterns to our servers. If you contact us, we use the information you provide only to respond to support, feedback, or compliance matters.

2. Images and Project Data

Images you import are used to generate bead patterns on your device. Generated patterns, edits, project drafts, and exported files are stored locally by default. PNG export includes optional project metadata and an optional source-image embed; if you share that PNG, the embedded data is shared with the file.

3. Permissions

  • System photo picker: used to choose images without full photo library read access.
  • Photo library add access: used to save exported PNG pattern images to Photos.
  • File access or sharing features: used for imports, local projects, and exports.

4. Third-Party Services

Doumen v0.1 does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, third-party tracking SDKs, or third-party account login by default. If these services are added later, this policy and App Store privacy details must be updated.

5. Retention, Deletion, and Rights

Local projects, exported files, and Photos content are managed by you on your device. Because Doumen v0.1 does not provide accounts or server-side storage, there is no remote account deletion process.

6. Children, Copyright, and Changes

Doumen is intended for general craft and creative use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. You are responsible for rights to images you import. If Doumen later adds network services, we will update this policy where required.
