Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Doumen is operated by Xuyu Tech. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Doumen mobile application, a local-first bead pattern maker and editor.

Doumen v0.1 is designed to work without registration, login, or image uploads. By using the app, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Policy. We may update this policy by revising the last updated date and posting the revised version in the app or on this page.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you create or provide locally

  • Images you choose through the system photo picker or file picker
  • Generated bead patterns, manual edits, project names, and local drafts
  • Export settings, including optional PNG project metadata and optional source-image embedding
  • Language and appearance preferences

2.2 Support information

  • Email address and message content if you contact support
  • Device model, iOS version, screenshots, or details you voluntarily include

Doumen v0.1 does not create user accounts and does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

3. Use of Data and Third-Party Services

Images and projects are processed locally on your device to generate, edit, save, and export bead patterns.

  • Apple system services: The system photo picker and photo-library add permission are used only for user-initiated import and export flows.
  • Local files: Project packages and exported PNG/PDF files are stored where you choose or in the app sandbox.
  • Support email: Information you send by email is used only to respond to support, privacy, or legal requests.

We do not sell personal data or use it for targeted advertising.

4. Storage, Security, and Retention

  • Local projects and drafts remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app.
  • Exported files are managed by you in Photos, Files, or any destination where you save or share them.
  • PNG files may include optional project metadata and, if enabled, the original source image; sharing that file shares the embedded data.
  • Support emails are retained only as needed to respond, maintain records, or meet legal requirements.

No transmission or storage method is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

5. Your Rights and Regional Notices

You may access, correct, export, or delete local projects using your device and app features. Because Doumen v0.1 does not provide accounts or server-side project storage, there is no remote account deletion process.

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, PIPL, APPI, or PIPA. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the applicable age threshold.

6. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact xuyutechnologies@outlook.com.
