## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Subscription Data:**
- Subscription names and service providers
- Billing amounts and currencies
- Billing cycles and next billing dates
- Subscription categories and payment methods
- Start/end dates and lifetime indicators

**Images for OCR Processing:**
- Screenshots or photos of subscription confirmations, invoices, or receipts
- Images are sent to our backend and forwarded to OCR providers for extraction
- We do not retain images after processing

**User Preferences:**
- Language, theme, notification, reminder, and currency settings

**Notification Data:**
- Reminder schedules you create
- APNs device token (iOS) if you enable notifications

**Purchase and Receipt Data:**
- App Store receipt data and transaction metadata for subscription verification

**Backup and Sync Data:**
- Subscription, reminder, and expense data synced to our backend for backup/restore and notifications

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Device Identifiers:**
- A randomly generated device ID stored on your device and used for backup/restore and notifications

**Service Logs and Metadata:**
- Basic request metadata such as request IDs, timestamps, and response status codes
- Our infrastructure providers may process network metadata (e.g., IP address) for security and reliability
- We do not use third-party analytics SDKs

### 2.3 Third-Party Data Processing

**OCR Service Providers:**
When you use OCR features, images are forwarded to third-party providers (currently Ark API or OpenRouter) to extract subscription details. We do not authorize these providers to use your data for training and do not retain images after processing; their handling is subject to their own policies.

**App Store & Payment:**
Purchases are processed by Apple. We receive receipt data and transaction metadata for verification.

**Infrastructure & Storage:**
We use cloud infrastructure providers (e.g., AWS) to operate backend services and store encrypted snapshots.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the Service
- Manage subscriptions and reminders
- Process OCR requests
- Enable backup/restore and notifications
- Verify purchases and entitlements
- Protect against abuse, fraud, and security incidents
- Comply with legal obligations

We do **not** sell your personal data or use it for targeted advertising.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
- Subscription data is stored locally on your device in a SQLite database
- Data is protected by your device's operating system security features (e.g., iOS data protection)

### 4.2 Server Backup
- We store encrypted snapshots of your subscription data to support backup/restore and notifications
- Snapshots are encrypted before storage when encryption is enabled in our backend configuration

### 4.3 Security Measures
We use reasonable security measures, including:
- HTTPS/TLS for data in transit
- Access controls and least-privilege policies
- Encryption for stored backup snapshots

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Local data** remains on your device until you delete it
- **Server snapshots** are overwritten by newer snapshots for the same device ID; older snapshots may persist until replaced or deleted upon request
- **Notification tokens/schedules** are retained until updated or removed
- **OCR images** are not retained by SubSnap after processing
